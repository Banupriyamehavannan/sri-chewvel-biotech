About Sri Chewvel Biotech

Sri Chewvel Biotech is a specialist plant tissue culture company founded by Mr. P. Deivasigamani, who brings over 10 years of in-depth expertise in high-volume plant production and market supply.

Our main lab and Head Office is located in Hosur, Krishnagiri Dist., Tamil Nadu. We also operate a branch office and additional production unit in Mathithoppu, ensuring wide distribution and capacity to meet large orders.

We utilize strict sterile protocols and a nutrient-rich medium to grow cells, tissues, or organs in a controlled, aseptic environment. This technique ensures that plants are multiplied rapidly, remain disease-free, and grow uniformly.

Our Commitment:

  • Quality Control: Strict aseptic lab protocols and documented quality assurance.
  • Expert Team: Led by experienced microbiologists and plant tissue culture scientists.
  • Scalability: Current annual capacity of 4 million plants, with planned expansion across both units.
